Major Records

  • Fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, 12,000 and 14,000 ODI runs
  • First player to score 50 and 51 ODI centuries (Most ODI centuries ever)
  • Fastest ever to 25,000 international runs (all formats combined)
  • Most centuries in chases in ODIs
  • Most runs in a single IPL season (973 runs in 2016)
  • Most runs in IPL history (8,509 runs as of 2025)
  • Only player with 8 IPL centuries
  • ICC ODI Player of the Year: 2012, 2017, 2018, 2023
  • Fastest to 4,000, 5,000 & 6,000 T20I runs
  • World Cup winner (U19, T20, ODI) captain for India
  • 30 Test centuries (7 double centuries), Highest Test score: 254*
  • Led India in most Test wins overseas as captain
  • Most International runs for India as a non-opener (25,000+)
  • Most successful chase batsman in ODI history
  • Most ICC Player of the Tournament awards (World Cups)
